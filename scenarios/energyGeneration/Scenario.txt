Energy is traded in exchange where generating companies participate to sell the energy they generate.
The spot price is based on the bid and ask price submitted.
For a generation company, the profitability is dependent on their ability to bid as low as possible, however
maintaining the profitability.


Here we are assuming the generating compay has wind, solar , coal, gas as source electricity generation capabilities.

To bid low, the generating company should be able to predict the demand every hour and also predict the how much they can 
generate using cheaper options (like solar, wind) and fulfill the remaining gap using coal and gas, minimizing opportunity 
lost and over production.
